Hello there!

I'm Creighton Higgins, and I build things on the Internet. I am skilled, enthusiastic, practical, curious, and heavy into full-stack development.

My design sensibility is based on building beautiful, useful tools that get out of your way when it's time to get to work.

I've been building websites professionally for over 16 years, and developing Internet applications for a dozen, mostly using Perl in open-source environments.

I also have keen interests in design and UX/UI, am delighted to see the lines between front-end and back-end technology soften and fade, and get hot ears any time someone says "full stack". I also get the grins from coding in Ruby, unraveling Drupal hooks, and designing with grids and SASS.

If you've got a project you'd like to discuss, don't hesitate to contact me. Let's talk about what you want to get done, and what sorts of beautiful tools we can build to do it.
